What is Media Design?

In general, digital media designers are trained in motion graphics, screenwriting, video editing, digital audio production and video production.


Most of these professionals are versed in some form of animation or motion graphics.


Graphic Designers Vs Media Designers

Digital media designers create their products in a digital or electronic format as opposed to graphic designers, who apply graphic imaging to both digital and tangible platforms.
